Our Mission is Language Literacy

The Bablebaby Story

Bablebaby was founded by two parents who wanted to raise multilingual children but struggled to find quality language instruction. We believe that playtime and childcare should involve educational enrichment activities.

That's why we created an online platform to connect families like yours with qualified multilingual babysitters from around the world. Our sitters transform ordinary childcare into an immersive language learning experience!

Our Vetting Process

We undertake robust vetting and screening to ensure that only the best sitters join our community:

  • Identity and background verification
  • Video interviews in their non-native language
  • Reference and experience checks
  • Ongoing parent ratings and reviews

World-Class Language Instruction

Our sitters utilize proven techniques like songs, stories, games, and activities to seamlessly teach vocabulary, pronunciation, grammar, and conversation skills.

We continuously monitor child progress and provide supplemental learning materials to parents like flashcards, books, and more based on their proficiency level.
